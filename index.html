Symptoms of High Blood Sugar
  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores
  • Significant Weight loss
  • Frequent infections
Symptoms of Low Blood Sugar
  • Hunger
  • Shaking
  • Sweating
  • Rapid Heartbeat
  • Headache
  • Dizziness
  • Confusion and difficulty concentrating
If you are suffering from the above symptoms, please seek for medical attention immediately!
